# Daily Notes — Tell Us How You Played, in Your Own Words

## In Plain English (start here)

Most of your reps happen out on the course, court, or field — not in front of a
camera. **Daily Notes** is the quick way to capture those days. After any round,
match, game, or practice you answer **"How did you play today?"** with a 1–5
feel rating, optionally type a few words ("sliced it off the tee and topped a
couple"), and SwingVantage reads the **faults out of your own language** and adds
them to your cross-sport [Athlete GI](athlete-general-intelligence.md) profile.

No launch monitor and no video required. When the same issue keeps showing up
across your days, it gets flagged as a **pattern** worth a dedicated fix.

> **Honesty note:** your feel rating is a **self-report**, clearly labeled
> low-confidence — it never poses as a measurement. The faults are read straight
> from the words you wrote, nothing invented.

---

## Where it lives

- **Full page:** **Daily Notes** under **Progress** in the sidebar, or `/notes`.
- **One-tap shortcut:** the **"How did you play today?"** card on your **Today**
  dashboard — tap how you played and you're done.

Works for all seven sports (golf, tennis, pickleball, padel, baseball, slow-pitch
and fast-pitch softball).

---

## How to use it

1. Open **Daily Notes** (`/notes`) or tap the dashboard card.
2. Pick **how you played** today (1–5).
3. *(Optional)* type a few words about what happened, in plain language.
4. Save. That's it — the note is stored on your device and folded into your profile.

What happens behind the scenes:

- A deterministic text parser maps everyday phrasing (e.g. "sliced it",
  "rolled over", "double fault", "popped it up") to **honest fault tags**.
- Those tags feed your **Athlete GI** player profile, where faults that recur
  across days are surfaced as patterns your plan can target.

---

## Your data, your control

Everything you write stays yours. Export or delete your Daily Notes anytime from
the **Data Center** (`/data`); they're also included in your backup so they move
with you between devices.
