# SwingVantage — Brand Guide

## In Plain English (start here)

This is the short rulebook for how **SwingVantage** should look and sound. If you're
writing a page, an email, a social post, or a flyer, skim this first so everything feels
like the same product. The one-line idea: *SwingVantage gives an athlete a smarter vantage
point on their swing — a clear view of what to fix, and a plan to fix it.*

---

## Name

- The product is **SwingVantage** — one word, capital **S** and capital **V**, no space.
- Don't write "Swing Vantage", "Swingvantage", "SV App", or the old name.
- The short monogram is **SV** (used in the app badge, app icon, and favicon).
- Domain: **swingvantage.com**. Contact: **support@swingvantage.com**.

## What SwingVantage is

An AI-powered swing-improvement platform for **golf, tennis, baseball, slow-pitch softball,
and fast-pitch softball**. An athlete uploads a swing video (or imports launch-monitor data),
and SwingVantage returns the single highest-impact thing to fix, sport-specific drills, and a
practice plan — free, private by default, no account required to start.

## Brand meaning

SwingVantage gives athletes a clearer, smarter **vantage point** on their swing — helping
them *see* what matters, *understand* what's happening, and *improve* with confidence.

## Personality

Intelligent · premium · clear · coach-like · encouraging · performance-oriented ·
family-accessible · trustworthy · technically advanced without being intimidating.

## Taglines

Pick the one that fits the context; don't force the same line everywhere.

- **Primary:** *See your swing. Understand your edge.*
- Your swing, from a smarter vantage.
- The smarter way to analyze and improve your swing.
- AI swing coaching for every athlete.
- Analyze smarter. Practice better. Improve faster.

Keep the **"Free AI swing analysis"** hook on conversion surfaces — free-first is the
go-to-market strategy, so don't drop the word "free" from headline/SEO copy.

## Voice — do / don't

**Do:** be clear and confident; name one priority fix, not ten; keep disclaimers but phrase
them warmly ("pairs with a coach," not "this isn't real coaching"); speak to athletes,
parents, and coaches alike.

**Don't:** use generic AI buzzwords; overpromise pro results; sound like a toy or a gimmick;
imply SwingVantage replaces a qualified coach or gives medical advice; remove the
heuristic-estimate / youth-safety / not-a-coach-replacement notices (reword, don't delete).

## Visual tokens

- **Brand green:** `#1a3a2a` (primary), `#2d5a40` (light) — see `apps/web/src/config` /
  `globals.css` theme tokens.
- **Monogram:** white **SV**, heavy weight, on the green gradient.
- Generated assets (icons + Open Graph share image) come from
  `scripts/generate-brand-assets.mjs` → run `npm run generate:brand` after any mark change.
- The current marks are clean code-generated placeholders. If a designed logo replaces them
  later, update that script (or the PNGs in `apps/web/public/`) and keep the SV monogram.

## Sports positioning (one line each)

- **Golf** — swing path, face control, tempo, sequencing, contact tendencies.
- **Tennis** — stroke mechanics, timing, racket path, body rotation, repeatability.
- **Baseball** — bat path, load, timing, hip-shoulder separation, contact position.
- **Slow-pitch softball** — timing, barrel control, launch direction, power transfer.
- **Fast-pitch softball** — bat speed, swing sequence, contact quality, consistency.

Keep the product **multi-sport** unless a page is specifically about one sport.
