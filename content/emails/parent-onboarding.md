Hi there,

Thanks for using SwingIQ to support your young athlete. A few things we want
you to know up front, because youth safety matters to us:

- **You're in control.** We recommend a parent or guardian manages uploads and
  reviews results for athletes under 18.
- **Privacy by default.** Youth data is never made public by default, and we
  don't share videos publicly.
- **Keep it positive and simple.** For young hitters, fewer cues and shorter,
  fun sessions beat long, technical ones.
- **SwingIQ is a practice aid, not a coach or medical professional.** For any
  injury concern or advanced work, see a qualified coach.
